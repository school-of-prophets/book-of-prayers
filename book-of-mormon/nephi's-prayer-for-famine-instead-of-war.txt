chapter: Nephi's Prayer For Famine Instead Of War

chapter-sum: Which he prayed as the war with the Gadianton Robbers waxed sorely in the lands of the Nephites.
chapter-sum: As recorded in Helaman 11:4 OPV
chapter-sum: Or Helaman 4:4 RAV

. O Lord, 
. do not suffer that this people shall be destroyed by the sword;
. 
. but, O Lord, rather 
. let there be a famine in the land 
. to stir them up in remembrance of the Lord, their God; 
.
. and perhaps they will repent
. and turn unto thee.
